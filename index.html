About

Built on Real Experience

JR Tech Industries was founded by Jacob Rodriguez — a U.S. Marine Corps veteran with a background in data systems administration, IT networking, and security technology.

After years of working in technical support and systems environments, the goal was simple: bring that level of discipline and real-world experience to local homes and small businesses that deserve better than overpriced, impersonal IT service.

Every job is handled with the same standard — clear communication, honest pricing, and work that's done right the first time.
